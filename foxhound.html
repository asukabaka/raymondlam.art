SYNOPSIS

My work reimagining of the iconic series Metal Gear Solid in a cyberpunk near future setting. These illustrations drawn in Photoshop for KawaiiSauce, an apparel brand specializing in Japanese Anime and JDM culture. Graphic and design work was done in both Photoshop and Illustrator for use in merchandise like apparel and computer acessories.
